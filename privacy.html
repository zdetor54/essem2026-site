Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the European Summer School on Eye Movements (ESSEM 2026, "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, affiliation, and other details you provide when applying or contacting us.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage data collected via cookies or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process applications and manage event participation.
  • To communicate with you regarding the event or your queries.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on your consent, our legitimate interests in organizing the event, or to comply with legal requirements.

4. Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with the ESSEM organizing committee and trusted service providers (e.g., email or web hosting) solely for the purposes described above. We do not sell your data or share it for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely and retain it only as long as necessary for the purposes stated. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where applicable).
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at essem@uni-bonn.de.

7. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to understand website usage and improve our services. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact
